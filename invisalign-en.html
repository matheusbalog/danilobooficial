Invisalign®


Invisalign® is a modern orthodontic treatment that uses clear, removable aligners to correct crooked or misaligned teeth. It offers discretion, comfort, and convenience, allowing you to eat and brush your teeth normally.

Invisalign®

Pros and Cons

PROS

  • Orthodontic Correction with maximum comfort
  • Discretion is the watchword
  • Oral hygiene care made easy
  • No Dietary Restrictions
  • Predictability at the highest level
  • Fewer trips to the dentist

Cons

  • Need for discipline
  • Initial discomfort that decreases over time
  • Cuidados de Higiene Oral facilitados
  • May not be effective in complex cases
  • Price: Invisalign® may be more expensive
  • Beware of the risk of loss or damage
